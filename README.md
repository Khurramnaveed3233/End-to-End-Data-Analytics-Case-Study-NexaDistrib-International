# 🚚 Global Supply Chain Performance & Optimization Analytics

## 📌 Project Overview
Designed and implemented an **end-to-end supply chain analytics solution** to identify operational inefficiencies and improve business performance across procurement, inventory, and delivery.

👉 Objective: Transform raw operational data into **actionable business decisions** with measurable impact.

---

## 🌍 Business Context
A global distribution company operating with:

- 20+ Suppliers  
- 8 Warehouses  
- 100+ Customers  
- Multi-region supply chain operations  

👉 Challenge: Lack of visibility across supply chain stages leading to inefficiencies and revenue loss.

---

## ❌ Key Business Problems

The organization was facing critical operational challenges:

- ⏱️ **Supplier delays** impacting delivery timelines  
- 📦 **18% products** at risk of stockout  
- 🚚 **Slow fulfillment cycles** (avg 6.2 days, some >10 days)  
- 📉 Lack of revenue visibility across regions  
- 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Customer profitability not clearly defined  
- 🏭 Warehouse utilization imbalance  
- 🚛 Carrier performance inconsistency  
- 💰 Dead stock locking working capital  

👉 Business Impact:
- Lost sales opportunities  
- Increased operational cost  
- Poor customer experience  

---

## 🗂️ Data Model

Designed an **end-to-end relational data model**:

Suppliers → Purchase Orders → Warehouses → Inventory → Customers → Sales → Shipments  

👉 Enabled full visibility across the supply chain lifecycle  
👉 Supported cross-functional analytics (procurement → delivery)

---

## 🧠 Analytical Approach

### 🎯 Objective:
Solve 8 core business problems using data

### 🛠️ Tools:
- SQL Server  
- Power BI  
- Excel  

### ⚙️ Techniques Used:
- CTEs for multi-step analysis  
- Window Functions for ranking & trend detection  
- Aggregations for KPI computation  
- Time-based analysis using date functions  

👉 Focus: Move from **data reporting → decision intelligence**

---

Developed a **4-page executive dashboard**:

1. Executive Summary (Revenue, Orders, Profit, Delivery KPIs)  
2. Inventory & Warehouse Analysis  
3. Supplier & Procurement Performance  
4. Sales & Delivery Performance

## 💡 Key Insights (Data-Driven)

### 🔴 Supplier Performance Gap
- Gold suppliers: **91% on-time delivery**  
- Bronze suppliers: **58% on-time delivery**  

👉 33% performance gap = major operational risk  

---

### 🔴 Inventory Risk
- **18% products** low/out of stock  

👉 Direct impact:
- Lost sales  
- Customer dissatisfaction  

---

### 🔴 Delivery Inefficiency
- Avg fulfillment: **6.2 days**  
- Peak delays: **>10 days**  

👉 Indicates process bottlenecks and poor logistics planning  

---

### 🟡 Revenue Concentration Risk
- Top **10% customers contribute 48% revenue**  

👉 High dependency risk → unstable revenue stream  

---

## 🧠 Business Recommendations

### 📦 Procurement Optimization
- Shift towards high-performing suppliers  
- Reduce dependency on low-performing vendors  

### 🔄 Inventory Automation
- Implement automated reorder points  
- Reduce stockouts and excess inventory  

### 🏭 Warehouse Optimization
- Redistribute stock based on demand patterns  
- Improve warehouse utilization efficiency  

### 🚛 Logistics Optimization
- Select high-performing carriers  
- Monitor SLA-based delivery performance  

### 💰 Working Capital Optimization
- Identify slow-moving inventory  
- Reduce dead stock  

---

## 📈 Expected Business Impact

- 🚀 **+20% improvement in on-time delivery**  
- 📉 **50% reduction in stockouts**  
- 📈 **10–15% increase in revenue**  
- 💰 Significant cost reduction  
- ⚡ Improved operational efficiency across supply chain
